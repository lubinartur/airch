# Today

## Definition

**Today** is not a dashboard.

It is the place where AIRCH decides what matters today.

It should not show everything. It should reduce the day to one primary truth.

---

## Core Question

> What deserves my attention today?

---

## What Today Does

The Reasoning Engine runs every morning across all domains — finance, health, projects, calendar, memory, open loops, active threads — and compresses everything into one primary signal.

Today is the output of that compression.

The user should not have to scan. AIRCH has already done that.

---

## Feeling

Opening Today should feel like reading the first sentence of a letter written by someone who knows your life — and who has already decided what you need to hear.

Calm. Spacious. Already ordered.

Not: here is everything.
But: here is what matters.

---

## Structure

**Primary thought**
One statement. The most important thing AIRCH has to say today. Could be a signal, a question, a warning, or an observation. Never more than 2–3 sentences.

**Focus**
One area or action that deserves primary attention. Drawn from the primary thought.

**Supporting context** (optional, collapsed by default)
If relevant — brief signals from other domains. Only shown if they support or contextualize the primary thought. Never just listed for completeness.

**Open threads**
Threads that are active and touch today's focus — surfaced quietly, not as a list.

---

## Principles

- One primary thought. Everything else recedes.
- AIRCH speaks first. The user does not arrive to a blank canvas.
- If there is nothing genuinely important, Today says so — calmly.
- Tasks appear only if they support the main focus. Not as a to-do list.
- Metrics appear only as meaning, never as raw data.
- No widgets competing for equal attention.

---

## Today Is Not

- a task list
- a calendar view
- a KPI dashboard
- a morning report with widgets
- a notification center
- a feed

---

## What Feeds Today

- Reasoning Engine output (primary)
- Active Threads
- Calendar signals
- Finance anomalies or upcoming obligations
- Health signals
- Project momentum or stalls
- Open Dialogue threads
- Direction context (what the user is trying to become)

---

## Empty State

If AIRCH has no signal worth surfacing today, Today is quiet.

Not empty in the visual sense — but calm.

Perhaps: a date. Perhaps a single line like "Nothing requires your attention today."

No filler. No encouragement. No suggestions to explore other sections.

Quiet is the message.
