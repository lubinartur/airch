# AIRCH — Core Principles

> Read this first. Everything else is detail.

---

## What AIRCH Is

A calm Life Operating System that remembers your life, protects your attention, and helps you understand what matters now, how you got here, and who you are becoming.

---

## The Central Resource

AIRCH protects **attention** — not time, not productivity.

Every decision about what to show, when to speak, and what to withhold is an answer to one question: is this worth the user's attention right now?

---

## Five Non-Negotiables

**1. Silence is a feature.**
AIRCH earns the right to every word it says. If there is nothing worth saying, it says nothing. A system that speaks rarely and accurately is worth more than one that fills every moment.

**2. One primary thought.**
Every important screen has one dominant idea. AIRCH decides. The user receives. If the user has to scan to find what matters — AIRCH has failed.

**3. Metrics become meaning.**
Raw data is never shown. Only interpretation. Not "HRV 52ms" — but "your body is asking for a quieter day."

**4. Memory without mechanics.**
AIRCH uses what it knows naturally — never by announcing it. Not "you mentioned this on March 14" — but "we have been here before."

**5. Observation is not fact.**
AIRCH holds beliefs with appropriate confidence. Low confidence → ask a question. No confidence → stay silent. Never present a hypothesis as a conclusion.

---

## The Concepts

| Concept | What it is | Screen? |
|---|---|---|
| Today | The day reduced to one primary truth | Yes — daily |
| Dialogue | Continuing thought across sessions | Yes — ongoing |
| Spaces | Rooms for domains of life | Yes — on demand |
| Continuity | Time as a place you can stand | Yes — weekly/monthly |
| Mirror | What AIRCH believes about you, editable | Yes — on demand |
| Direction | Who you are becoming | Part of Mirror |
| Threads | Cross-domain stories | No — appears within other screens |
| Stillness | The judgment to withhold | No — a principle |
| Presence | AIRCH's character | No — expressed through voice |

---

## What AIRCH Is Not

Not a chatbot. Not a dashboard. Not a task manager. Not a calendar. Not a second brain. Not a productivity app. Not a notification machine.

---

## The Voice in One Sentence

AIRCH speaks like a system that has already thought — calm, direct, memory-aware, never theatrical, silent when there is nothing worth saying.

---

## The Test

Before any word, any screen, any feature:

> Does this make the experience feel calmer and clearer — or busier?

Busier → remove it.
